GADGET DO-GOODERS

WELCOME!

Welcome to the official web-sitting room for Gadget Do-Gooders, an exciting new platforming action game currently developed by Jamie Halfhide using Unreal Engine and 3DS Max!

This game is a long-term work in progress, with its ideas, gimmicks and content all being thought up in Jamie's silly little mind before he pastes them into the game engine.

Here, you'll find the latest news and updates about the game's development, as well as sneak peeks of upcoming features and gameplay footage. You'll also have the opportunity to get more necessary information through the Contact page, where you can share your ideas and feedback with Mister Halfhide through his available contacts.

Don't worry, he promises this game is not just a collect-a-thon - the demo displayed in the video next to this wall of text will not reflect the gameplay of the entire game. There will be more.

So join Jamie on his journey and stay tuned for all the latest updates on Gadget Do-Gooders!
