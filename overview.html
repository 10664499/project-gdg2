GADGET DO-GOODERS

OVERVIEW

Gadget Do-Gooders is a platforming game about levelling up weapons, taking on bosses, and processing through helping the people you care about. The game works both solo, locally and online - you may want to playthrough on your own, or with friends working aside you!

Throughout the game, you follow the main character Zachary Halt, a rising do-gooder in a world where gadgets dominate construction and self-dominate, who must use his newfound pack for good and learn the responsibilities of the new life on his shoulders.

Something that is clearly not a cakewalk with criminals and villains roaming, using their own gadgets for destruction.
